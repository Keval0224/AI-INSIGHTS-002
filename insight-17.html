Large Language Models: Powering the Next Wave of AI

LLMs like GPT-4 and Gemini are setting new benchmarks in natural language understanding and generation. Their applications range from chatbots to content creation.

What are Large Language Models?

Large Language Models (LLMs) are deep learning models trained on massive text datasets. They can generate human-like text, answer questions, summarize documents, and more.

Why Does It Matter?

LLMs are transforming industries by automating content creation, powering conversational AI, and enabling new research in linguistics and knowledge discovery.

  • Prompt engineering
  • Zero-shot and few-shot learning

Example

AI is now writing news articles, generating code, and even composing poetry that is often indistinguishable from human work.
