Robotics in Industry 4.0

Robots are no longer confined to assembly lines. With AI, they are becoming collaborative partners in manufacturing, logistics, and even customer service.

What is Robotics in Industry 4.0?

Industry 4.0 refers to the fourth industrial revolution, where smart factories use AI-powered robots, IoT devices, and automation to optimize production and supply chains.

Why Does It Matter?

AI-driven robots increase efficiency, reduce errors, and enable flexible manufacturing. They can work safely alongside humans and adapt to changing production needs.

Subtopics

  • Cobots (collaborative robots)
  • Warehouse automation
  • Smart factories and digital twins
  • Predictive maintenance

Example

AI-driven robots are now working alongside humans in car manufacturing, handling tasks from welding to quality inspection, and even logistics.
