Edge AI: Intelligence at the Device Level

Edge AI brings machine learning models directly to devices, reducing latency and improving privacy. This is powering smart cameras, wearables, and autonomous vehicles.

What is Edge AI?

Edge AI refers to running AI algorithms locally on hardware devices, rather than relying on cloud servers. This enables real-time decision-making and reduces the need for constant internet connectivity.

Why Does It Matter?

Edge AI improves privacy, reduces bandwidth costs, and enables applications in remote or mobile environments. It is crucial for IoT, robotics, and autonomous systems.

Subtopics

  • TinyML (machine learning on microcontrollers)
  • On-device inference
  • Federated learning
  • Smart sensors and cameras

Example

AI-powered security cameras can detect threats in real time without sending video to the cloud, ensuring both speed and privacy.
